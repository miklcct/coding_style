Spelling

Rule

Names MUST be spelt out in full, except when spelling industry standard acronyms (e.g. HTTP, DNS, IP) and units (e.g. m, km, dpi).

Explanation

All names must be spelt out in full form, without missing a single letter. Industry standard acronyms (such as HTML) and units (such as km, dpi) are considered words on their own which may be used directly.

Examples

AcceptableUnacceptable
expressionexpr
doctordr
viewervwr
HTMLHTM
iOS
kmkms

Note: kms in the last example is unacceptable because SI units cannot be pluralised.
