Non-function

Rule

Non-boolean non-function names MUST be a noun.

Explanation

All non-functions, including types, variables, constants, must be a noun unless it is a boolean.

Examples

Variables

  • lunch_box
  • car
  • people

Constants

  • MAXIMUM_SIZE
  • TOLERANCE

Types

  • Animal
  • Person
